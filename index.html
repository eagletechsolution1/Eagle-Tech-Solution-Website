About Eagle Tech Solution

Eagle Tech is a dynamic software company established by five passionate software engineers. Founded just a month ago, the company is committed to creating impactful software solutions tailored to local and global markets.

Unlike traditional open-source organizations, Eagle Tech Solution operates as a for-profit enterprise with a mission to innovate, compete, and provide value through high-quality tech products and services.

The company's core objectives include:

  • Designing and selling competitive digital solutions across various domains of software development.
  • Delivering training programs to help bridge the technology skills gap across Ethiopia.
  • Promoting digital literacy and reducing technology-related illiteracy in underserved communities.
  • Supporting and accelerating Ethiopia's journey toward digital transformation.
