About Eagle Tech Solution

Eagle Tech Solution is a dynamic software company established by five passionate software engineers with a vision to drive digital transformation in Ethiopia and beyond. We combine technical expertise with deep understanding of local market needs.

Our Mission

To deliver cutting-edge technology solutions that empower businesses, educational institutions, and government agencies to achieve their digital transformation goals while fostering local talent and expertise.

Core Objectives

  • Design and sell competitive digital solutions across various domains of software development
  • Deliver training programs to bridge the technology skills gap across Ethiopia
  • Promote digital literacy and reduce technology-related illiteracy in underserved communities
  • Support and accelerate Ethiopia's journey toward comprehensive digital transformation

Our Approach

Unlike traditional open-source organizations, Eagle Tech Solution operates as a for-profit enterprise with a strong commitment to social impact. We believe sustainable technology solutions come from balancing innovation with practical business models that ensure long-term viability and continuous improvement.
